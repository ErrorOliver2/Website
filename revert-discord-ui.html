Download Vesktop which has Vencord preincluded (It's a client mod)

Once you have logged into Vesktop, go to settings then go to Plugins. Search "experiments" and turn it on. It may ask you to reload Discord.
Do that then go back to settings, scroll down and click on "Experiments" In it, search for "desktop visual refresh" and turn it off. (set it to "not eligible")
ps if you're interested in more features out of Vencord (you want more than the fix), go check out the Plugins in Vencord settings and see what you want!

Alternatives to discord:
If you want to leave Discord because of the changes it's making, I suggest the following services: Revolt, Matrix, and Spacebar Chat (Work In Progress).
