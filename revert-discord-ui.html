Download Vesktop which has Vencord preincluded (It's a client mod... for technical people: Vesktop is a Desktop wrapper that has Vencord included)

Once you have logged into Vesktop, go to settings then go to the "themes" tab which is in the "Vencord" category (scroll down the settings list to see it) Once you are on the "themes" tab, click on "online theme" and paste this in: 
https://raw.githubusercontent.com/davart154/DTM-18/main/dtm-18.theme.css
